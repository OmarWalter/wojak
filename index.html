The Lore

What is WOJAK?

Wojak. Feels Guy. The bald MS Paint man who's been making the internet feel something since 2010.

First posted on a Polish imageboard, it was then shared to a German imageboard by user "Wojak." It took off on 4chan soon after and spread to every corner of the internet. By 2011, it was already going viral everywhere with stories posted with the template "that feel when…"

Since then, Wojak has grown to become his own meme universe. There's the Zoomer, the Doomer, the NPC, the Soyjak, the Chudjak, Brainlets, and so many other variations. He's often drawn with his close friend Pepe the frog. There's really no internet culture without Wojak.

Shortly after the launch of the memecoin PEPE in 2023, this meme lore was brought onchain.Wojakcoin (WOJAK) is the first community-led memecoin on Ethereum for the Wojak meme.

Wojakcoin isn't just meme lore, he's also core crypto lore. Wojakcoin is forever.
